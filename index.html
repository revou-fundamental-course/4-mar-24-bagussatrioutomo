This calculator is intended for use by adults only (pregnant or breastfeeding women are not advised to rely on BMI readings). No specific actions should be taken based on the BMI value, except consulting with qualified individuals such as a doctor.

The calculator will provide you with an overview of the comparison of your body weight to general values. Body Mass Index (BMI) is calculated as your weight (in kilograms) divided by the square of your height (in meters) or BMI = Kg/M2.
